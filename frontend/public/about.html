Our Journey

From a Small Farm to Sangli's Trusted Dairy

It all began in 2009 when Amol Jadhav, a third-generation farmer, decided to modernise his family farm and bring hygienic, traceable dairy products directly to urban households. What started with just 8 cows and a bicycle delivery route has grown into a full-scale dairy operation serving 500+ families.

Today, Jadhav operates a clean, FSSAI-certified dairy unit where every litre of milk is tested for quality before packaging. Our animals are grass-fed, ethically raised, and never given artificial hormones.

We believe that the best dairy comes from healthy, happy animals and honest farming practices. That philosophy hasn't changed since day one — and it never will.

Our Values

What We Stand For

Every decision we make at Jadhav is guided by these core principles.

Purity First

No artificial additives, preservatives, or colours. Our products are as close to nature as possible.

Animal Welfare

Our cows and buffaloes live in spacious, clean sheds and graze on open pastures. Happy animals give better milk.

Fair Practices

We pay fair wages to our staff and maintain transparent pricing for our customers. No hidden costs.

Sustainability

Biodegradable packaging, water recycling, and solar-powered operations — we care about the planet.

Quality Assurance

Every batch is tested for fat content, purity, and hygiene before it leaves our facility. Zero compromise.

Community First

We support local farmers, employ from the community, and contribute 2% of profits to rural education.
