FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a daily milk subscription?
Simply register your account, go to the Customer Panel, and click on "Subscriptions". Select your preferred product, quantity, and delivery time. Your delivery will start from the next morning!
What time is morning milk delivery?
Morning deliveries are made between 5 AM and 8 AM, depending on your location. Evening deliveries are between 5 PM and 7 PM.
How is billing done for subscribers?
Monthly invoices are generated automatically based on your daily delivery quantity. You can pay at the end of each month via cash, UPI, or online transfer. View your invoice in the Customer Panel.
Can I pause or cancel my subscription?
Yes! You can pause or cancel your subscription anytime from your Customer Dashboard. Changes take effect from the next delivery day.
Are your products FSSAI certified?
Absolutely. Jadhav Dairy is FSSAI certified and all our products undergo regular quality testing. We maintain strict hygiene standards at every step.
Do you deliver on Sundays and holidays?
Yes! We deliver 365 days a year including Sundays and holidays. Fresh milk doesn't take a day off, and neither do we! 🥛
